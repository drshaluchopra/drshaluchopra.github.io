Manipulative Realities

Viral Propaganda of Digital Deception

When seeing is no longer believing.

Fake viral videos and deepfakes have broken the link between seeing and believing. On platforms with no gatekeeper, the same engine of manipulation quietly reshapes entertainment, religion, and politics. The book is built on original doctoral research across three generations, fifteen case studies, and interviews with senior journalists.
